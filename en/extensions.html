Catalog of extensions

In our catalog, specifically designed for your online store on the OpenCart / ocStore CMS, you will find modules and modifiers developed to enhance the functionality and efficiency of your online business

Additional description of categories

Allows you to enhance categories with unique texts. Hide lengthy texts. Flexible settings for attractive and informative category pages

HTML-Content PLUS

Displays HTML code on the website with visibility control. Hide and reveal text. Customization for different customer groups

HTML-Content PRO

Allows you to create and customize text blocks on the website. Unlimited number of blocks. Flexible customization to suit your needs and design

Units measurement product

Allows you to set your own unit of measurement for the product price

Reply to reviews

Allows you to respond to customer reviews and send them an Email with the answer

JS Readmore for product description

Enables you to manage long product descriptions with a text hiding feature for compactness and readability

JS Readmore for category description

Allows you to manage long category descriptions. Create compact and informative category pages with a text hide and expand feature

Information blocks

Allows you to display information pages (About Us, Shipping Information, Security Policy, Terms of Agreement, etc.) as blocks with a brief description

Custom templates

Allows you to set a specific template for products, categories, manufacturer (brand) or information pages
