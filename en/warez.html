What is «Warez»?

Warez is illegally distributed software with copyright infringement, hacked and modified source code.

Usually, the code is modified so that it can be used for free or sold at a lower cost, but as a «bonus», users of hacked modules and templates receive various kinds of malware — trojans, miners, hidden ads, etc. After all, hackers are trying not just like that, but to somehow monetize their activities.

Websites that distribute cracked software often encode their files using the eval() function. You can read about the dangers of using this function in the official PHP documentation. Or even worse, they write unencoded code, which at first glance does not pose any threat, but with skillful handling you can get full access to the store and information.

Warning!

Using eval() can be very dangerous because it allows arbitrary code to be executed. Use of this feature is not recommended. If you are absolutely convinced that there is no other way to reproduce the required functionality, pay particular attention to the data processing exceptions.

How to distinguish sites that distribute warez?

Unfortunately, the owners of varezniks are becoming more and more impudent and are trying to make money from their visitors, taking advantage of their ignorance. To do this, they make their websites look like legal sites, so it is very easy for an inexperienced person to get confused among all these resources.

Understanding that you have encountered a wareznik is not so difficult if you know their characteristic features:

  • In the sections with additions you see the words «nulled», «cracked», «without ioncube», «shared»;
  • The presence of a «subscription», «vip account» and other mechanisms that provide access to all add-ons at once;
  • You are required to take some action to download the file (like, subscribe);
  • Add-on downloads are limited to those who have recently registered, but restrictions can be removed for a fee;
  • All add-ons were uploaded by several users, have one author with the login «admin» or have no authors at all;
  • The link to download the add-on is under the spoiler (hide), to open it you need to pay the administration;
  • «Shares» are organized for add-ons, that is, a group purchase of one add-on;
  • After downloading the file, you discover that it is password protected, and to find out the password, you need to pay the administration;
  • The site copies the design or uses some of its elements from official sites;
  • Links to download add-ons lead to free file hosting services, but not to the server of the resource itself.

If you are going to buy or download a module or template from the site, it is recommended that you first check the site for danger or simply write to us.

Consequences of using warez:

  • You support software thieves and motivate them to engage in such activities;
  • You lose technical support;
  • You often receive an old broken / partially working version of the module;
  • Your site becomes easy to hack;
  • Foreign code, unknown files, links appear on your website;
  • Your users are exposed to intrusive advertising;
  • Spam is sent through your website on your behalf;
  • All ongoing payment transactions and personal data can fall into the hands of fraudsters;
  • And most importantly, it relates more to humanity. By buying or downloading such stolen modules and templates, you take away «bread» from developers, thereby simply taking away the desire to create legal free or paid, but inexpensive modules.

Upon official purchase you receive:

  • You receive the author's guaranteed support for the operation of the module or template if any conflicts or errors arise;
  • The absence of viruses and backdoors is a guarantee of the stability of your business;
  • The ability to improve your store without unnecessary problems and checks;
  • You can get critical updates for the next versions of the module or template for free;
  • You help developers make even more useful and inexpensive solutions. In addition, if the authors don’t have money, then there will be no modules, including even in Warez (after all, Warez appears from modules, and if they don’t exist, then there won’t be Warez);
  • You become a member of a large community and will be able to get help with your store and other issues.

Conclusion!

Always be careful where you buy or download the solution for your online store. And also choose competent specialists. If you don’t trust a specialist, always ask for confirmation of the legality of purchasing modules and templates and check it yourself. This will save you from serious problems and downtime in your business in the future.
